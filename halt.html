HALT CREATION

Location:

Keywords:

Overview

HALT CREATION is now a futurism fashion apparel based in British Columbia, Canada. It started off as a side project during Wilhelm’s 3rd&4th year of undergraduate studies in University of Victoria. With no clear outline of the brand’s direction, audience, it was an exploration of design relates to his personal interests and experience.

Time Constraint

An interesting aspect of the project is that there is a time constraint.

Inspired by Wilhelm's language learning experience, all products of the first collection #FFCC66 reflect the industrial characteristic of the German language. You can find a tag attached to the garments, written “mindestens haltbar bis 30 04 2020.” Its direct translation in German is “expired date April 30th, 2020.”

This special arrangement indicates that HALT CREATION is set to be finalized/terminated at the end of Wilhelm's undergraduate degree, which is set to be the 30th of April in 2020.

The philosophy behind this time constraint is to create a sense of urgency for project realization. This tradition remains till today with variations in languages.

#

HEX CODE SYSTEM

HEX code, a system widely used to describe composition of a certain colour in a specific colour space, such as the a 2D webpage on the internet. As information technology becomes an inseparable part of our daily lives, the collections of HALT CREATION are categorized into 7 different colours, that include

FFCC66 (YELLOW), FF0000 (RED), 0000FF (BLUE), 00FF00 (GREEN), 000000 (BLACK), FFFFFF (WHITE), CCCCCC (GREY)

Each of the colours has its own unique characteristic. This concept later developed into experimental sountracks for the HALT themed interactive art show, known as “Create Your Own Reality (CYOR)“.
