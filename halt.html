Overview

HALT CREATION is now an online high-end fashion apparel based in British Columbia, Canada. It started off as a side project during Wilhelm’s 3rd&4th year of undergraduate studies in University of Victoria. With no clear outline of the brand’s direction, audience, it was an exploration of all relevant to my personal interests and experience.
